SPC Chart Exercise

Instructions: in your groups...

  • Each pick a different hospital.
  • Click on the button for your chosen hospital.
  • The dummy data will change (each hospital is different).
  • Download the Excel Single XmR tool using the link.
  • Paste into the Excel tool.
  • Change the settings to fit the data format and to show the chart properly.
  • Re-calculate where appropriate.
  • Add annotations if desired.
  • Export of copy and paste the created X-chart to a blank powerpoint.
  • Write a brief appropriate SPC-language description of the data.
  • Do the same for each of the three metrics.
  • In your groups show each other your charts and read out your descriptions.
  • If time, each group will be asked to feedback on one chart.
