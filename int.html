Making Data Count


There are a wide range of Making Data Count resources, tools, and training opportunities, all developed in England and available to access for free by anyone!


Join our collaboration site, where you can become a member and easily access everything you need to support your Making Data Count journey.


To join the Making Data Count Futures collaboration space, simply provide your email address here. Once registered, you’ll gain full access to all available resources, including:


Tools
A variety of tools are available to help you create Statistical Process Control (SPC) charts. These tools include user-friendly Excel templates, SQL scripts, solutions in R and Python, and a Microsoft-certified Power BI custom visual. You can explore the tools here.


Training Sessions and Materials
We offer a series of 10 training sessions, each lasting an hour and delivered every six weeks. You can join live sessions or choose to watch recordings at your convenience. Live sessions can be booked through the Events Calendar, and all training materials and recordings are available in this folder.
